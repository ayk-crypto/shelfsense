Update ShelfSense pricing/plan selection customer experience.

Current issue:
Basic and Pro are showing “Coming Soon”, and Business plan is not visible. This looks bad from customer point of view and makes the product appear incomplete.

Required changes:

1. Remove “Coming Soon” badges/buttons from Basic and Pro plans.

2. Show all customer-facing plans:
- Free
- Basic
- Pro
- Business

3. Plan actions:
- Free: button text should be “Start Free”
- Basic: button text should be “Choose Basic”
- Pro: button text should be “Choose Pro”
- Business: button text should be “Contact Sales” or “Choose Business”

4. Since payment gateway is not live yet:
When a customer selects any paid plan, do not block them with Coming Soon.
Instead:
- Save selected plan in backend
- Create subscription with status PENDING_ACTIVATION or PENDING_PAYMENT
- Show a professional message:
  “Your selected plan has been saved. Our team will contact you shortly to activate billing.”
- Allow customer to complete signup/onboarding after selecting the plan, but keep paid features limited until admin activates the subscription.

5. For Free plan:
- Activate immediately
- Mark subscription as ACTIVE
- Redirect customer to dashboard

6. Add Business plan to pricing/plan selection UI with realistic limits:
Business Plan:
- Unlimited or high item limit
- Multiple locations
- Multiple users
- Advanced reports
- Priority support
- Custom onboarding
- Button: “Contact Sales” or “Choose Business”

7. Admin side:
- Admin should be able to see customers with PENDING_ACTIVATION / PENDING_PAYMENT status.
- Admin should be able to manually activate selected paid plan.
- Admin should be able to update billing cycle and expiry date manually.

8. UI wording:
Replace “Coming Soon” with:
- “Manual activation available”
or simply remove badge completely.

9. Customer UX:
After selecting paid plan, show:
Title: “Plan Selected”
Message:
“Thank you. Your selected plan has been saved. Since online payment is not enabled yet, our team will contact you for billing and activation.”

Button:
“Continue to Dashboard”

10. Make sure plan selection progress reaches 100% after a user selects any plan, including paid plans with pending activation.
